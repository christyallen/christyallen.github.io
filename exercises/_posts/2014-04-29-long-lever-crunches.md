**Cycle 1, 2, 3, 4**

switch legs each cycle

- on mat - one leg bent and foot on floor 
- other leg extended
- arms extended above head
- crunch the two together  

**MODIFY**: 1 arm; only lift arm pit and one shoulder

**Advanced** w/ 5 lb weight
